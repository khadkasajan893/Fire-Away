ABOUT US

OUR STORY

The first Fire Away pizza truck opened on Main Street in Chicago in 2008. Our owner Lesli Yvonovich inherited the first truck from his uncle and struggled to find a way to put it to good use. It was only after he flunked out of the University of Chicago's Law School did he realize that he wanted to be an entrepreneur.

As a 22 year-old college dropout with very little money, Lesli took what he had along with a fairly developed cooking skill and came up with Fire Away.

His idea was to be a cheap, mobile source of food for college students like him while also providing high quality restuarant-style meals. Traveling all across college campuses, Lesli's pizzas became a hit!

After just two years on the road, Lesli had increased his inventory of trucks from one to seven and had brought his father in to help him run the buisness.

OUR FAMILY

We at Fire Away have been a family-run organization since the beginning, and we've only grown in size. When Lesli's wife Khris joined the team in 2011, she reworked the company's social network prescence, bringing delicious brick-oven pizza to people of Chicago!

Thanks to her hard work, the word of our values has spread across the state of Illinois, bringing people from all over to come and meet us!

"FAMILY FIRST IS OUR MOTTO" - KHRIS YVONOVICH

By having Lesli's parents Robert and Kendra helping us, customers say that they feel more connected when they visit one of our trucks, which is our main goal when serving you, besides of course making great pizza!

OUR CREED

At Fire Away, our creed is made up of three simple rules; familiarty, accessibility, and freshness. We make sure that our customers get the best ingredients, at the best time from the best people.

All the ingredients that we use are exclusively organic and raised on free range farms. Before it comes to you it passes the most rigorous of tests to make sure that it holds up to what you ask for.

Being a mobile food company, accessibility is something that has been a part of our buisness model since the beginning. In addition to our Main Street campus, we regularly update our media feeds with the location of each truck that day, making sure you can come to us no matter where life takes you!

"NO MATTER WHERE LIFE TAKES YOU"
